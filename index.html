BasketBall Game

The game was created by James Naismith, a physical education instructor, on December 21, 1891, at the International Young Men's Christian Association (YMCA) Training School (now Springfield College) in Springfield, Massachusetts.

This game is played between two teams where each team has to score goal in the goalpost of other team and defend their own goalpost. In general there are 5 players in each team(including guards,forwards and center) but larger teams are preferred in professional teams and the number can go up to 12-15 members.

To score a goal you need to dunk the ball in the opposing team's goalpost. The goal is a tower which is having a cylindrical hole with a net.

The farther you score a goal from the tower the more points your team gets.

  • A basketball team can have a maximum of five players on the court.
  • Player substitutions can be made at any time and there is no restriction on the number of substitutions made.
  • A ball can travel through dribbling or passing.
